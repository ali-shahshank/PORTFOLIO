Case Study

Nexcom Ai: End-to-End Design and Front-end Development of an Intelligent Support Agent, Boosting Customer Satisfaction by 20% and Cutting Response Times by 30%

Role

Lead Web-Designer & Front-end Developer

Client

Nexcom, a leading Canadian ISP and telecom provider, delivers high-quality services nationwide. With a growing customer base, it continually scales to meet evolving demands and industry standards

Business Challenge

NexComm sought to elevate customer service while alleviating demands on human agents by implementing an intelligent, high-performance solution for troubleshooting, billing, account management, and general inquiries.

Strategy

Leverage market research to design and develop a high-performance AI agent that seamlessly elevates Nexcom’s customer support experience while driving measurable improvements in customer satisfaction.

Solution

To elevate NexCom's customer support, a high-performance, content-aware AI support solution was required. An AI Support agent designed to efficiently handle a wide range of services, including billing, account management, ticketing, technical support, and general inquiries.

Goals

User Goals

Provide quick, efficient support solution for, billing, account management and troubleshooting through an intuitive AI support agent. Reducing response time and enhancing application usability.

Business Goals

  • Streamline customer support with automation.
  • Boost customer satisfaction 20%.
  • Cut response times by 30%.
  • Reduce operational costs.

Research & Insight

Exploring AI's Role in Transforming Business and Workforce Adaptability

Awareness & Adaptability

Ai Chatbots are becoming more prevalent in the recent years. In the upcoming years Ai will be an essential part of operational efficiency in business. According to usertesting.com 97% of knowledge workers are aware of Ai Chatbots and other major Large Language Models such as ChatGPT, Gemin, Meta Ai and others. Almost 60% of these knowledge workers are using them occasionally.

Companies must focus on both functionality and business value to meet the growing needs of today’s workforce.

User Classification

User reactions to AI-powered customer support chatbots show varying levels of engagement. Around 20% of users are highly dedicated, relying on these chatbots for efficient issue resolution and quick support. 

Approximately 37% of businesses utilize chatbots for customer support interactions. Additionally, 69% of consumers are willing to use chatbots for instant responses to their queries, including self-service issues.

Our strategy focuses on delivering a reliable, user-friendly AI support chatbot, addressing the growing demand for quick, efficient service. By prioritizing seamless interactions and scalability, Nexcom Ai is designed to meet both user and business needs effectively.

Design Process

Crafting Intelligent Solutions Through User-Centered Innovation

Visual Identity

To craft an intuitive, user-centric experience, we needed to develop a unique and compelling visual identity for the AI application, ensuring it resonated with users while reinforcing Nexcom's brand values.

Logo Design

The logo seamlessly integrates Nexcom's iconic logo mark, symbolizing interconnected nodes within the telecommunications network. This design element reflects the brand’s core identity and its strong foundation in delivering reliable and connected solutions.

The wordmark combines "NexCom" with "Ai," representing the advanced AI support application. This pairing highlights Nexcom's commitment to innovation, blending its legacy in telecommunications with cutting-edge AI technologies to create a distinctive and forward-thinking visual identity.

Typography

Balancing Minimalism and Innovation.

Primary

Roboto

Roboto was chosen for its clean and modern design, offering a professional yet approachable aesthetic that aligns seamlessly with Nexcom Ai's innovative identity. Its high readability makes it an ideal choice for both headings and primary text, ensuring clarity in every user interaction.

Secondary

Inter

Inter was chosen for its legibility at smaller sizes, offering a clean, highly readable design ideal for body text and paragraphs.

System Design

We primarily relied on Agile Framework to guide our SDLC process, ensuring adaptability and continuous improvement.

01

Discovery and Planning

The process began with understanding project goals, user needs, and technical requirements. Product backlog is created, prioritizing features for development.

02

Wireframing & Prototyping

Low-fidelity wireframes and interactive prototypes were developed to visualize the structure and functionality, ensuring the design aligns with user expectations before moving to full development.

03

Iterative Design & Development

Design and development occured in short, focused sprints, with each iteration addressing specific features or pages.

04

Collaboration & Feedback

Regular communication through daily stand-ups and sprint reviews ensured collaboration between design, development, and stakeholders.

05

Testing & Deployment

Thorough testing ensured browser compatibility, accessibility, and performance. Incremental deployments are carried out to maintain a seamless user experience.

06

Evaluation & Iteration

After launch, feedback is collected, and performance metrics are analyzed. The team refines the design and features based on insights.

Technologies & Implementation

Implementing System Design and architecture through cutting-edge technologies.

Design

We utilized Figma as our primary design and ideation tool, enabling efficient wire-framing, mockups, and prototyping while ensuring seamless collaboration and iterative refinement. This approach allowed us to create polished visuals and design assets, enabling user-centered designs.

Front-end

A comprehensive Front-end solution was implemented by utilizing HTML, CSS, Bootstrap and Java-Script.

Back-end

Back-end was implemented with Python, using Numpy for data processing, PyTorch for model development and training, and Flask for server-side rendering with built in routing and high performance api's, ensuring a robust solution.

Database and Architecture

MongoDB was an ideal choice for asynchronous requests and real-time data processing, paired with AWS services to delivered a high performance scalable Ai solution.

Results & Impact

Achieving Efficiency and User Satisfaction through Advanced AI Integration.

  • Billing issue resolution times were reduced by 40%, allowing customers to receive prompt and accurate billing clarifications.
  • Automated account management boosted efficiency by 25%, with 60% of tasks, like plan changes and payments, handled by AI, reducing manual workload and enabling seamless self-service.
  • 95% of technical support requests processed within the application were resolved on the first interaction, reducing response times by 35% and improving user satisfaction while minimizing agent dependency.
