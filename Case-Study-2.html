A Comprehensive Visual Identity & Web-Design Transformation for Tripwise.

Refining the brand's essence with a modernized visual identity and a seamless, user-focused web design that elevates customer experience and engagement.

Client

TripWise is a travel agency specializing in corporate and personal travel packages. They offer customized itineraries and seamless booking services, earning a strong reputation within the travel industry.

Challenge

TripWise needed a modern web app to unify customer engagement, strategic marketing, and bookings. Their current application did not reflect their brand values and marketing approach.

Solution

TripWise required a complete redesign of their proprietary web app and a refreshed visual identity, creating a seamless platform that unified customer engagement, marketing, and booking for an enhanced user experience.

Approach

Strategy: Develop an intuitive, user-friendly web design adhering to specific guidelines, with a refreshed visual identity. Integrate interactive elements prioritizing customer engagement and marketing.

Process

Identify Business Problem

Collaborated with stakeholders to define challenges, gather design requirements, and craft tailored solutions for impactful results.

Analysis & Documentation

Documented findings into sitemaps, process flows, and user experience maps, building a data-driven project roadmap.

Design & Prototype

Created user-centric wireframes, prototypes, and interactive mockups in Figma/Adobe XD, ensuring intuitive, brand-aligned designs.

Developer Handoff

Validated and optimized designs, incorporating custom code as needed, and delivered tested files for seamless development handoff.

Web Design Strategy

Our design strategy focused on intuitive navigation, seamless functionality, and alignment with the refreshed brand identity.

Web-Design

Tripwise application redesign leveraged a user-focused approach, revamping key pages such as Home, Destinations, Services, and Packages. The design seamlessly aligned with the new brand voice and visual identity, incorporating modern aesthetics, intuitive navigation, and engaging interactive elements for a cohesive user experience.

Admin Dashboard Implementation

The admin dashboard redesign focused on integrating the new visual identity, requiring comprehensive content restructuring and systematic updates to enhance functionality and alignment with the refreshed brand.

Mobile Responsiveness

By leveraging libraries such as Bootstrap5 we ensure all of our web solutions are responsive across various devices and cross platform compatible by default.

Developer Collaboration & Handoff

The handoff process began with thoroughly optimizing and rigorously testing all design files to guarantee compatibility and functionality across a variety of devices and screen sizes. Detailed specifications were meticulously prepared, including comprehensive style guides, reusable component libraries, and clear interaction flows, ensuring the development team had a well-structured and efficient roadmap for implementation.

Collaboration with developers was integral throughout the project, involving consistent communication to address technical challenges, adjust designs for responsiveness, and maintain strict adherence to established design standards. Regular design reviews and iterative feedback sessions were conducted to refine the final output, ensuring every element met the intended vision. This process resulted in a pixel-perfect implementation that delivered a seamless, user-friendly experience aligned with the refreshed brand identity.

Results & Impact

Driving Engagement & Market Success

Measurable Impact

  • Increased Engagement and Bookings: Achieved a 35% rise in engagement and 25% more bookings within the first quarter.
  • Enhanced Usability and Design: Improved usability and modern aesthetics earned positive client feedback.
  • Strengthened Market Position: Positioned TripWise as a market leader with a stronger competitive edge.

TripWise redesign was an enriching experience that allowed me to tackle complex challenges, from creating a new visual identity to ensuring seamless functionality across the platform. I was truly inspired by the impact of our work and the valuable lessons learned along the way.
